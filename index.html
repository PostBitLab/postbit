Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions about our projects, mission, and how we work.

What is Postbit Labs?

Postbit Labs is a product-first startup focused on building open-source tools in API development, land intelligence, and geospatial AI for public and enterprise use.

Is your work open-source?

Yes, all our projects are open-source and hosted on GitHub. You can explore the repositories, contribute, or deploy them for your own use.

Can I contribute to your projects?

Absolutely. We welcome contributions through GitHub. Check our contribution guidelines in each repo to get started.

What technologies do you use?

We primarily use FastAPI, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, React, Python, and various geospatial and AI libraries to build our platforms.

Is Geomatrix available for governments?

Yes, Geomatrix is designed to assist governments in mapping land parcels, overlaying environmental data, and planning for the future.

Where can I see your project roadmap?

Each GitHub repository includes an Issues and Discussions section where we outline upcoming features and plans.
